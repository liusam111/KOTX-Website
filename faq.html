FAQ

Want to know more about KOTX?

What's KOTX?

KOTX is UCSD’s only K-pop community! We’re both a social organization and an all-inclusive dance team. Our mission is to provide a warm and welcoming environment for all K-pop fans!

Y'all have events?

Yes! We have a wide variety of social events which are all meant to be a bonding time with fellow KOTX members/K-pop fans, and we also host K-pop dance workshops, where we teach a small portion of K-pop choreographies! To come to the social events and to the workshops, there’s nothing required! There’s no fee, no sign-ups, no nothing. Just bring yourself!

What's a KOTX membership?

Everyone is welcome to come to our events, but paid members do have benefits. You’re able to have first dibs on carpool situations, you get priority on events that can only have a certain number of people (like karaoke night), you can join the dance team, and you can film a dance cover. KOTX Staff always promises that your membership fees go back to the members. There’s no point in having the money if it’s not spent on our members. Also, if you are having ANY sort of financial difficulties with the fee, please also talk to staff about it, money should not and will not get in the way of you joining the club.

  • To pay the membership fee, please feel free to come to our events or come to our quarterly info nights so you can meet with our treasurer and they can assist you. Any other questions can be directed to DMs through any of KOTX’s social media pages

How do you join the dance team?

  • Do you need to be a paid member? Yes
  • Do you need dance experience? No, absolutely not.
  • Do you need to come to our workshops? Technically no, but it’d be really good if you came
  • Do you need to show up to practice? Yes

When's practice?

A calendar will be provided if you’re on the dance team.

How do you do a cover?

The performances listed in our dance practice videos playlist and in our live performances playlist are all big sets put together by our dance committee and performed by all of our members. You just have to join the dance team.

Just to quickly clarify:

We’re not a Korean cultural club, you DO NOT have to be korean to join KOTX.

Any other questions?

You can direct message KOTX on any of our social media pages, you can come to our quarterly info nights to learn more, you can come to any of our social events to talk to us, and you can come to any of our dance workshops to talk to us. Please feel free to message us with any questions you might have about KOTX.
