ABOUT KOTX

KOTX is THE K-Pop community at UCSD. Founded in 2015, KOTX’s mission is to provide a welcoming environment and to spread Korean Culture through the art of K-pop. Everyone's Invited! Always having fun and going crazy, Let’s get KOTX.

This is UCSD’s one and only K-pop dance and social organization and we are dedicated to providing a home for the K-pop fans of UCSD. We’re all-inclusive, meaning everyone’s invited! Essentially, we’re a bunch of K-pop nerds who get together and share our love of K-pop. We’re a big, crazy, chaotic, welcoming, friendly family. Others may think we’re weird for liking K-pop, but who cares, we’ve got each other and that’s all that matters.

UCSD'S ONLY K-POP SOCIAL ORG AND DANCE TEAM

Social Org

Our Social Aspect is for everyone to enjoy! Our events are meant for you to with fellow K-pop fans in other ways, and are also meant for the club members to grow closer together. We want to grow and support the K-pop loving community, especially with the stigma surrounding being a K-pop fan. We want to help spread the Hallyu Wave by providing a warm and safe space for you to be yourself. Essentially, one big crazy K-pop family.

Dance Team

Our Dance Team is All-Inclusive, meaning that everyone's allowed to be on the dance team! No prior experience required, we want our dance team to be a safe space for everyone to dance to their favorite K-pop songs without the stigma of dancing to K-pop. You can not only grow as a fan, but also grow as a dancer.
